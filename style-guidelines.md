# Style Guidelines
The next properties belongs to the general aspect.

## Colors
  * White: #FFF
  * Blue: #1B9CFC
  * Red: #FF3F34
  * Dark: #1E272E
  * Gray: #ACACAC
  * Light: #E3E3E3

## Fonts
  * Heading & Paragraphs: Poppins 

## Font Size
  * XXLarge: 48px
  * XLarge: 28px
  * Large: 22px
  * Base: 18px
  * Small: 16px

## Borders
  * 50% or 16px


## Breakpoints
  * Mobile: 320px
  * Tablet: 720px
  * Desktop: 1120px
